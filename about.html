Mission

We are determined to be one of the proficient, admired and picked shipping and logistics partner to viable clients and principals. We have strong grasp over the business and hence, deliver effective solutions that not only meet your business expectation but also add value at every interface.


Vision

We have a clear vision to lay foundations of a potent, best quality and service driven platform in the market, delivering logistics management solutions to corporate enterprises in the most productive manner.


Core Expertise

We possess comprehensive skills in the handling, loading, shipping and discharge of a wide range of dry bulk cargo. We are well-versed with the special terminal operations, the vessel make and storage conditions and also the equipment used. We are well qualified to offer proficient, safe and valuable handling of bulk and break-bulk cargo vessels.


We possess comprehensive project cargo management knowledge and expertise. We are always on a look out to designate trained professionals and mission critical equipment to deliver a trouble-free experience from beginning to end for heavy or out of dimension cargo. Besides this, we also provide freight forwarding selected value-added services along with a technologically driven work environment making sure that we serve principals and customers alike – Quality service that complements global standards.


We provide a complete package of shipping services at all major ports for different categories of cargo. We are one of the leading Custom Brokers (CB), licensed stevedores, warehouse owners/providers for both liquid and dry cargo, shipping agents’ forwarders/ affreightment agents, fumigator of all types of agro-products and warehouses operating at all western and eastern ports of India.


Shivam Seatrans Private Limited has its own fleet of trailers, hydra/loaders, forklifts etc. Permanent field staff of more than 180 personnel covering Western and Eastern Indian ports to provide excellent services and ensure the cargo reaches its destination with complete care. Our working philosophy is to ensure that our customers always come first. We aim to become business partners in progress for our clients, offering expert advice and solutions to all importing and exporting communities and their logistic needs.
